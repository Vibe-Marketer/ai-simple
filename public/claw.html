04. Architecture

Why This AI Employee Works

Built on the CLAW architecture — the reason ours keep running when everyone else’s quietly die.

No Context Rot

Smart memory system keeps what matters and ignores noise, so performance doesn’t degrade in long conversations.

Specialist Guild, Not a God Bot

A small team of specialist agents, each with scoped skills and memory, rather than one generalist great at nothing.

Deterministic Skills

Critical work handled by tested, code-based skills — not brittle prompts — so you can trust it in production.

Security-First by Design

Separate environment, separate accounts, local or private memory options. Your data stays under your control.

Cost-Efficient Model Routing

Routine work uses cheaper models; complex reasoning uses premium ones — keeping “gas fees” under control.

06. FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need to be technical?

No. You approve the plan; we handle setup, deployment, and maintenance. Zero terminal access required from you.

Will this touch my personal accounts or machines?

By default, no. Everything runs in its own environment with its own accounts. That’s the entire point of our isolation-first architecture.

What if OpenClaw changes or breaks?

That’s what your management plan covers. We track upstream changes, patch issues, and keep things stable so you don’t have to.
