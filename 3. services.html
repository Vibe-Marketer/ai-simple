FAQ

Common Questions

How quickly can we start?

We typically onboard new clients within 3-5 business days. Once the contract is signed and the deposit is paid, we schedule the kickoff immediately.

Do you offer custom packages?

Yes, if our standard plans don't fit your needs, the 'Custom' tier allows us to scope a specific project with fixed deliverables and timelines.

What tech stack do you use?

We specialize in modern stacks: React, Next.js, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, and Node.js. For CMS, we often use Sanity or Contentful.

Can I pause my subscription?

Absolutely. If you don't have enough work for a full month, you can pause your subscription and resume it when you have more requests.
